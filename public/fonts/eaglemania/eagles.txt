Eaglemania Truetype Font for Windows

2001 Iconian Fonts - Daniel Zadorozny

http://www.iconian.com

This is a dingbats fonts.  Upper and lower case A-Z (as well as numerals and extra punctuation) contain identical but flipped glyphs of over 40 images related to eagles and hawks.
